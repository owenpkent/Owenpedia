## Shift Guides

### [Morning Shift Guide](./morning-shift-guide.md) - 7:00 AM - 12:00 PM
**Complete guide for morning shift caregivers** including:
- Wake up and initial care procedures
- Transfers (toilet, shower, wheelchair)
- Medications and feeding setup
- Equipment cleaning
- Household responsibilities
- End of shift checklist

**Key:** Allow 2 hours for morning care routine. Owen will talk you through it.

### [Evening Shift Guide](./evening-shift-guide.md) - 5:00 PM - 10:00 PM + Overnight
**Complete guide for evening shift caregivers** including:
- Arrival and check-in
- Dinner preparation (bring ~1 cup of food to share)
- Evening activities and projects
- Bedtime routine (9pm)
- Night monitoring (10pm-7am)
- Morning handoff

**Key:** Baby monitor must be on with volume up. Owen may wake 1-5 times during night.

## Key Information

### Morning Routine Essentials
- **Start Time:** 7am (or 2 hours before first engagement)
- **Duration:** Approximately 2 hours
- **Note:** Owen will talk you through his routine

### Night Care
- **Baby monitor must be on** with volume up
- Owen may wake **1-5 times** needing assistance
- Common needs: covers, suction, turning, position adjustments

### Shift Completion
All shift and household responsibilities must be completed before leaving at noon.
