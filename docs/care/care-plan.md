## Shift Structure

### Morning Shift: 7:00 AM - 12:00 PM
**Primary Focus:** Complete morning care routine, household responsibilities

See **[Morning Shift Guide](./morning-shift-guide.md)** for detailed procedures including:
- Wake up and initial care
- Transfers (toilet, shower, wheelchair)
- Medications and feeding setup
- Equipment cleaning
- Household tasks
- End of shift checklist

**Key Points:**
- Allow 2 hours for morning care routine
- Owen will talk you through his routine
- No breakfast - Owen does not eat in the morning
- All shift responsibilities must be completed by noon

### Evening Shift: 5:00 PM - 10:00 PM (+ overnight monitoring)
**Primary Focus:** Dinner, evening activities, bedtime routine, overnight care

See **[Evening Shift Guide](./evening-shift-guide.md)** for detailed procedures including:
- Arrival and check-in
- Dinner preparation and sharing meal
- Evening activities and projects
- Bedtime routine (9pm)
- Night monitoring (10pm-7am)
- Morning handoff

**Key Points:**
- Bring something yummy to share for dinner (~1 cup of food)
- Owen may wake 1-5 times during night needing assistance
- Baby monitor must be on with volume up while sleeping
- Be ready for morning shift handoff at 7am

## Mobility & Transfers

### Equipment
- **[Wheelchair](../supplies/supplies-reordering.md#wheelchair):** Rovi A3 with lateral tilt (Aspen Seating custom seat)
- **[Lift System](../supplies/supplies-reordering.md#patient-lift):** Patient lift with [universal mesh sling](../supplies/supplies-reordering.md#lift-sling) (head support)
- **[Shower Chair](../supplies/supplies-reordering.md#shower-chair):** For bathing support

### Transfer Protocol
1. Ensure [wheelchair](../supplies/supplies-reordering.md#wheelchair) is tilted back before transfer
2. Position [lift sling](../supplies/supplies-reordering.md#lift-sling) properly under patient
3. Use [patient lift](../supplies/supplies-reordering.md#patient-lift) for all transfers (bed, toilet, shower, wheelchair)
4. Always check [equipment](../supplies/supplies-reordering.md) before use
5. Never attempt manual transfers
