# Daily Care Guide

## Evening Routine - 6pm

- Turn on porchlight
- Feed Owen dinner
- Help Owen with projects

---

## Bedtime Routine - 9pm

- Brush Owen's teeth
- Take [melatonin](../medical/medications/melatonin.md)
- Fill up [humidifier](../medical/equipment.md#humidifier)
- Put Owen in bed and take off clothes and [catheter](../medical/equipment/catheter.md)
- If comfortable, assist Owen with sexual wellness device and leave room
- Rinse out [catheter](../medical/equipment/catheter.md) tube
- Clean out nose mask
- Turn off lights in office
- Empty pee jug and rinse out and pee mat
- Shut windows in office
- Turn off the fan in office
- Charge phone, [wheelchair](../medical/equipment.md#wheelchair), and [feeding pump](../medical/equipment.md#feeding-equipment)
- Turn on baby monitor and Activate if needed
- Open window as much as Owen would like it open
- Put [CPAP mask](../medical/equipment.md#cpap-mask) on Owen. Turn on 3 machines in this order - Left: [Ventilator](../medical/equipment.md#ventilator), Middle: [Humidifier](../medical/equipment.md#humidifier) (on "Low" with only one of the three lights on), Right: [Oxygen](../medical/equipment.md#oxygen-machine)
- Adjust pillows and covers as asked
- Put a pillow behind his back
- Put a striped towel under Owen's face to absorb any saliva during night
- Put eye cover on
- Make sure the baby monitors are on and linked (one in his bedroom next to bed, one in caregiver room on nightstand)

---

## After Bed Routine - 10ish pm

- Empty pee jug into toilet, rinse jug out, wipe outside of pee jug and mat down with a disinfecting wipe, wipe down any spots on floor around mat with a disinfecting wipe. Fill jug â…“ full of water and replace on office mat
- Turn off lights in living room, office, kitchen
- Finish any kitchen cleaning as needed

---

## Sleeping Routine - 10pm - 7ish am

- Go to his room when he calls out during the night. (Make sure baby monitor is turned on and volume is up while you are sleeping.)
- He usually wants any or all of the following: covers adjusted, suction, turned over, legs/arms adjusted, pillows adjusted
- He may wake up 1-5 times during the night and need assistance with these things

---

## Morning Routine - 7ish am (or 2 hours before his first morning engagement)

**Allow 2ish hours to complete Owen's morning routine. And he will talk you through his routine!**

Owen does not eat breakfast in the morning. If you eat breakfast, it is good to do it before waking him up in the morning.

### Wake Up
- Open curtains near bed, close window, turn on space heater
- Attach feeding tube to Owen's stomach Mickey button. Make sure it is pinched closed!
- Give 1 syringe full of Kate Farms box food. Give 1 syringe full of water
- Detach feeding tube

### Breathing Equipment
- Turn off [breathing machine](../medical/equipment.md#ventilator), [humidifier](../medical/equipment.md#humidifier), and [oxygen](../medical/equipment.md#oxygen-machine) in opposite order as in the night: Oxygen (right side machine), Humidifier (middle machine), Ventilator (left side machine)
- Take off CPAP mask

### Toileting & Showering
- Put Owen in sling and lift and bring him to toilet. Wait in bedroom for him to finish
- Transfer Owen from bed to [shower chair](../medical/equipment.md#shower-chair)
- Give 1 [Albuterol](../medical/medications/albuterol.md), 1 [Zyrtec](../medical/medications/zyrtec.md) and 2 squeezes per nostril of [Flonase](../medical/medications/flonase.md)
- Shower. Brush teeth and mouthwash in shower also. (Wear a swimsuit or clothes you don't mind getting wet as you are in the shower stall with Owen during his shower.) Clean stomach Mickey button area well with split sponge
- Transfer Owen back into sling and lift and then back to bed on top of a towel

### Personal Care
- If comfortable, assist with men's health and wellness routine
- Sponge bath
- Put on [catheter](../medical/equipment/catheter.md)
- Get dressed. Put catheter tube down inside right leg of pants

### Transfer to Wheelchair
- Unplug wheelchair, feeding machine
- Turn on and tilt [wheelchair](../medical/equipment.md#wheelchair) back
- Transfer Owen to sling and lift and then to wheelchair. Adjust clothes and catheter

### Feeding & Medication
- In Kitchen: Reattach feeding tube to Owen's stomach Mickey button
- Give Owen 6.6 mL [Evrysdi](../medical/medications/evrysdi.md) (use orange syringe) through a large syringe attached to his [feeding tube](../feeding/feeding.md). Followed by a tall beaker of water
- Fill food bag with feeding formula - 1 pouch of Liquid Hope. 1 pouch of water. 2 boxes of Kate Farms. 2 boxes of water. Fill feeding bag completely full right to the very top of bag. Make sure cap is fastened well or it can easily spill
- Attach feeding bag to carabiner on wheelchair. Attach to feeding machine. Put feeding bag in black bag. Turn on (keep the button pushed until all of the numbers stop changing). Prime until food gets close to tip. Attach to feeding tube and push "Run."
- Turn on computer

### Clean Up
- Clean all syringes, beakers with warm water (no soap)
- Empty [humidifier](../medical/equipment.md#humidifier) canister and leave out to dry in bathroom
- Empty [suction](../medical/equipment.md#suction-machine) canister and rinse with warm water and soap. Return to [suction machine](../medical/equipment.md#suction-machine). Or, wash in dishwasher as needed
- Make beds, tidy bathrooms, tidy bedrooms, tidy kitchen, etc.
- Make sure all Shift and House Responsibilities are completed
- Help Owen with any projects or tasks
