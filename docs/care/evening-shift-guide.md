# Evening Shift Guide

> **Shift Hours:** 5:00 PM - 10:00 PM  
> **Duration:** 5 hours

---

## Shift Overview

The evening shift focuses on dinner preparation and sharing a meal with Owen, evening activities and project assistance, and completing the bedtime routine. This shift includes overnight monitoring until the morning shift arrives.

**Important:** Owen will talk you through his preferences and routine.

---

## Arrival - 5:00 PM

### Check-In
- Touch base with Owen
- Look at shift tasks on the whiteboard for your PM shift
- Review any notes from the morning shift

### Initial Tasks
- Tidy and clean anything that needs it
- Unload dishwasher if clean
- Do Owen's laundry if needed
- Help Owen with any projects

---

## Evening Routine - 6:00 PM

### Dinner Preparation

**Turn on porch light**

#### Meal Guidelines
- Please bring something yummy to share with Owen for dinner
- Owen usually eats about **a cup of food** per evening
- He likes foods that are:
  - Softer textures
  - Easily cut into smaller pieces
- He enjoys good food!

### During Dinner
- Sit down and have dinner together with Owen
- Enjoy conversation and company

### After Dinner
- Do dishes from dinner
- Load into dishwasher
- Tidy kitchen

---

## Evening Activities - 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM

### Spending Time Together
- Hang out with Owen
- Help with any projects
- Watch movies/shows together
- Engage in conversation

### Household Responsibilities
Complete any household responsibilities that need to be done:
- See [Household Checklists](./household-checklists.md) for tasks
- Focus on items that weren't completed during morning shift
- Prepare for next day as needed

---

## Bedtime Routine - 9:00 PM

### Medications
Give:
- **[Melatonin](../medical/medications/melatonin.md)**
- **[Glycopyrrolate (Glyco)](../medical/medications/glycopyrrolate.md)**

### Brush Teeth
- Use toothbrush and toothpaste
- Toothbrush should be charging when not in use

### Humidifier Setup
1. Fill humidifier canister with cool tap water up to the fill line
2. Place in humidifier on bottom shelf next to bed
3. Make sure the 2 blue tubes are attached to the humidifier canister

### Equipment Management
**Charge:**
- [Wheelchair](../medical/equipment.md#wheelchair)
- [Feeding pump](../medical/equipment.md#feeding-equipment)

**Turn Off:**
- [Robot arm](../medical/equipment.md#robot-arm)
- [Wheelchair](../medical/equipment.md#wheelchair)
- [Feeding pump](../medical/equipment.md#feeding-equipment)

---

## Feeding Tube Care

1. Detach [feeding tube](../feeding/feeding.md) from the feeding bag tube
2. Remove [feeding tube](../feeding/feeding.md) from Owen's stomach Mickey button
3. Clean [feeding tube](../feeding/feeding.md) with a couple syringes filled with warm water (no soap)
4. Leave on paper towel to dry overnight in kitchen
5. Discard feeding bag (unless Owen says otherwise)

---

## Transfer to Bed

1. Use [sling and lift](../medical/equipment.md#lift-sling) to get Owen into bed
2. Remove clothes
3. Remove [catheter](../medical/equipment/catheter.md)
4. Discard "condom" [catheter](../medical/equipment/catheter.md)
5. Rinse longer [catheter](../medical/equipment/catheter.md) tube with warm water
6. Hang in shower to dry overnight

---

## Breathing Equipment Setup

### Suction Machine
- Make sure [suction machine](../medical/equipment.md#suction-machine) canister is empty or no more than half full
- Make sure there is water in hanging suction water bottle
- Add a splash of mouthwash to keep things clean
- Use as Owen requests

### CPAP and Breathing Machines

1. Open window as much as Owen would like
2. Put [CPAP mask](../medical/equipment.md#cpap-mask) on Owen
3. Turn on machines in this order (left to right):
   - **Left:** [Ventilator](../medical/equipment.md#ventilator)
   - **Middle:** [Humidifier](../medical/equipment.md#humidifier) (on "Low" - only one of three lights on)
   - **Right:** [Oxygen](../medical/equipment.md#oxygen-machine)

---

## Comfort Adjustments

- Adjust pillows and covers as asked
- Put a pillow behind his back
- Put a striped towel under Owen's face (absorbs saliva during night)
- Put eye cover on

---

## Baby Monitors

**Critical:** Make sure both monitors are on and linked:
- One in Owen's bedroom next to bed
- One in caregiver room on nightstand

**Volume must be up while you are sleeping**

---

## After Bed Tasks - 10:00 PM

### Pee Jug Care
1. Empty into toilet
2. Rinse jug out
3. Wipe outside and mat with disinfecting wipe
4. Wipe any spots on floor around mat with disinfecting wipe
5. Fill jug â…“ full of water
6. Replace on office mat

### House Closing
- Turn off lights in living room, office, kitchen
- Finish any kitchen cleaning as needed
- Ensure all tasks are complete

---

## Night Monitoring - 10:00 PM - 7:00 AM

### Sleep Routine

**Baby monitor must be on and volume up while you are sleeping**

### Responding to Calls

Go to Owen's room when he calls out during the night.

#### Common Requests
Owen may want any or all of the following:
- Covers adjusted
- Suction
- Turned over
- Legs/arms adjusted
- Pillows adjusted

#### Frequency
Owen may wake up **1-5 times** during the night and need assistance with these things.

### Being Present
- Stay within earshot
- Respond promptly to calls
- Maintain awareness throughout the night
- Ensure Owen's comfort and safety

---

## Morning Handoff - 7:00 AM

### Prepare for Morning Shift
- Be awake and ready when morning shift arrives
- Brief morning shift on any overnight events
- Share any concerns or observations
- Ensure smooth transition

---

## Important Notes

### Safety Protocols
- Always use [sling and lift](../medical/equipment.md#lift-sling) for transfers - never attempt manual transfers
- Ensure [wheelchair](../medical/equipment.md#wheelchair) is tilted back before transfers
- Check all [equipment](../medical/equipment.md) before use
- Make sure [feeding tube](../feeding/feeding.md) is properly cleaned and dried

### Communication
- Ask questions if unsure about any procedure
- Report any equipment issues immediately
- Document any concerns or changes in condition
- Communicate with morning shift about overnight events

### Owen's Preferences
- Owen will guide you through his routine - listen to his preferences
- Respect his privacy and dignity
- If comfortable, assist with personal wellness routines
- Maintain professional boundaries while being personable
