# Morning Shift Guide

> **Shift Hours:** 7:00 AM - 12:00 PM (Noon)  
> **Duration:** 5 hours

---

## Shift Overview

The morning shift focuses on Owen's complete morning care routine, which takes approximately 2 hours, followed by household responsibilities and project assistance until noon.

**Important:** Owen will talk you through his routine. Allow 2 hours to complete the morning care routine.

---

## Morning Routine - 7:00 AM

### Wake Up & Initial Care

1. **Prepare the Room**
   - Open curtains near bed
   - Close window
   - Turn on space heater

2. **Initial Feeding**
   - Attach feeding tube to Owen's stomach Mickey button (make sure it is pinched closed!)
   - Give 1 syringe full of Kate Farms box food
   - Give 1 syringe full of water
   - Detach feeding tube

3. **Turn Off Breathing Equipment**
   
   Turn off in this order (right to left):
   - **Oxygen** (right side machine)
   - **Humidifier** (middle machine)
   - **Ventilator** (left side machine)
   - Remove CPAP mask

---

## Transfers & Personal Care

### Toileting
1. Put Owen in [sling and lift](../medical/equipment.md#lift-sling)
2. Transfer to toilet
3. Wait in bedroom until finished

### Showering
1. Transfer Owen to [shower chair](../medical/equipment.md#shower-chair)
2. Give medications:
   - 1 [Albuterol](../medical/medications/albuterol.md)
   - 1 [Zyrtec](../medical/medications/zyrtec.md)
   - 2 squeezes per nostril of [Flonase](../medical/medications/flonase.md)
3. Shower Owen
   - **Note:** Wear a swimsuit or clothes you don't mind getting wet (you'll be in the shower stall with Owen)
   - Brush teeth and mouthwash in shower
   - Clean stomach Mickey button area well with split sponge

### Post-Shower Care
1. Transfer Owen back to bed on top of a towel
2. If comfortable, assist with men's health and wellness routine
3. Sponge bath
4. Put on [catheter](../medical/equipment/catheter.md)
5. Get dressed ([catheter](../medical/equipment/catheter.md) tube goes down inside right leg of pants)

---

## Transfer to Wheelchair

1. Unplug [wheelchair](../medical/equipment.md#wheelchair) and [feeding machine](../medical/equipment.md#feeding-equipment)
2. Turn on [wheelchair](../medical/equipment.md#wheelchair) and tilt back
3. Transfer Owen to [sling and lift](../medical/equipment.md#lift-sling), then to wheelchair
4. Adjust clothes and [catheter](../medical/equipment/catheter.md)

---

## Kitchen - Feeding & Medication

### Medication Administration
1. Reattach [feeding tube](../feeding/feeding.md) to Owen's stomach Mickey button
2. Give Owen **6.6 mL [Evrysdi](../medical/medications/evrysdi.md)** (use orange syringe to measure) through a large syringe attached to his [feeding tube](../feeding/feeding.md)
3. Follow with a tall beaker of water

### Prepare Feeding Formula
1. Fill food bag with:
   - 1 pouch Liquid Hope
   - 1 pouch water
   - 2 boxes Kate Farms
   - 2 boxes water
2. Fill feeding bag completely full right to the very top of bag
3. **Important:** Make sure cap is fastened well or it can easily spill

### Set Up Feeding Machine
1. Attach feeding bag to carabiner on wheelchair
2. Attach to feeding machine
3. Put feeding bag in black bag
4. Turn on (keep the button pushed until all of the numbers stop changing)
5. Prime until food gets close to tip
6. Attach to feeding tube and push "Run"

### Start the Day
- Turn on Owen's computer

---

## Morning Clean-Up Tasks

### Equipment Cleaning
- Clean all syringes and beakers with warm water (no soap)
- Empty [humidifier](../medical/equipment.md#humidifier) canister and leave out to dry in bathroom
- Empty [suction](../medical/equipment.md#suction-machine) canister and rinse with warm water and soap
  - Return to [suction machine](../medical/equipment.md#suction-machine), OR
  - Wash in dishwasher as needed

### Household Tidying
- Make beds
- Tidy bathrooms
- Tidy bedrooms
- Tidy kitchen

---

- [ ] Stove cleaned

### Project Assistance
- Help Owen with any projects or tasks he's working on
- Be available for assistance as needed

### Feeding Bag Refill

  - 2 boxes Kate Farms

---

## Important Notes

### Owen's Preferences
- Owen does **not** eat breakfast in the morning
- If you eat breakfast, do it before waking Owen up
- Owen will guide you through his routine - listen to his preferences

### Safety Protocols
- Always use [sling and lift](../medical/equipment.md#lift-sling) for transfers - never attempt manual transfers
- Ensure [wheelchair](../medical/equipment.md#wheelchair) is tilted back before transfers
- Check all [equipment](../medical/equipment.md) before use
- Make sure [feeding tube](../feeding/feeding.md) is pinched closed when attaching/detaching

### Communication
- Ask questions if unsure about any procedure
- Report any equipment issues immediately
- Document any concerns or changes in condition
