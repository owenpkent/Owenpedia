# Daily Routines

Quick reference guides for daily care routines. For complete detailed procedures, see the [Care Documentation](../care/README.md).

---

## Routine Guides

### [Morning Routine](./morning-routine.md)
Wake up through noon shift end (7:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
- Wake up and breathing equipment removal
- Toileting and showering
- Medications (Albuterol, Zyrtec, Flonase)
- Transfer to wheelchair
- Feeding setup and Evrysdi administration
- Morning clean-up tasks

**Time Required:** Allow 2 hours for complete morning routine

### [Evening Routine](./evening-routine.md)
Evening shift arrival through dinner (5:00 PM - 9:00 PM)
- Arrival and check-in
- Dinner preparation and sharing meal
- Evening activities and projects
- Household responsibilities

**Note:** Bring ~1 cup of food to share for dinner

### [Bedtime Routine](./bedtime-routine.md)
Preparation for sleep (~9:00 PM)
- Medications (Melatonin, Glycopyrrolate)
- Feeding tube care and removal
- Transfer to bed and catheter removal
- Breathing equipment setup
- Comfort adjustments

### [Night Routine](./night-routine.md)
Overnight monitoring and care (10:00 PM - 7:00 AM)
- Night monitoring with baby monitor
- Responding to Owen's calls (1-5 times per night)
- Assistance with positioning, covers, suction
- Morning handoff preparation

### [End of Shift Checklist](./end-of-shift-checklist.md)
Tasks to complete before leaving shift
- Kitchen and dishes
- Household tidying
- Equipment maintenance
- Shift handoff notes
