# Evening Routine

> **Shift Start:** 5:00 PM

---

## Arrival (5:00 PM)

- Check in and touch base with Owen
- Look at shift tasks on the whiteboard for your AM and PM shift
- Tidy, clean anything that needs it
- Unload dishwasher if clean
- Do Owen's laundry if needed
- Help Owen with any projects

---

## Dinner (6:00-7:00 PM)

### Meal Preparation

- Please bring something yummy to share with Owen for dinner
- He usually eats about **a cup of food** per evening
- He likes foods that are:
  - Softer textures
  - Easily cut into smaller pieces
- He enjoys good food!

### During Dinner

- Sit down and have dinner together
- Turn on porch light

### After Dinner

- Do dishes from dinner
- Load into dishwasher
- Tidy kitchen

---

## Evening Activities (7:00-9:30 PM)

- Hang out with Owen
- Help with any projects
- Watch movies/shows together
- Complete any [household responsibilities](../household/chores.md) that need to be done

---

## Transition to Bedtime

At approximately 9:30 PM, begin the [Bedtime Routine](./bedtime-routine.md).
