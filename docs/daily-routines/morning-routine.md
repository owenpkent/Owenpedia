# Morning Routine

> **Start Time:** 7:00 AM (or 2 hours before first morning engagement)

Allow approximately 2 hours to complete Owen's morning routine. Owen will talk you through it!

---

## Quick Start

1. Give Owen syringe of [Kate Farms](../feeding/feeding.md) food and water
2. Turn off breathing machines (see [Medical Equipment](../supplies/supplies-reordering.md))
3. Transfer to shower → toilet → shower → bed → wheelchair
4. Give medications and prepare [feeding formula](../feeding/feeding.md)

---

## Detailed Steps

### Wake Up (7:00 AM)

- Open curtains near bed, close window
- Turn on space heater
- Attach [feeding tube](../feeding/feeding.md) to Owen's stomach Mickey button *(make sure it is pinched closed!)*
- Give 1 syringe of Kate Farms box food + 1 syringe of water
- Detach feeding tube

### Breathing Machines Off

Turn off in this order (right to left):
1. **Oxygen** (right side machine)
2. **Humidifier** (middle machine)  
3. **Ventilator** (left side machine)

Then remove the CPAP mask.

### Transfers and Bathroom

1. Put Owen in sling and lift → transfer to toilet
2. Wait in bedroom until finished
3. Transfer to shower chair

### Medications

Give the following:
- 1 Albuterol
- 1 Zyrtec
- 2 squeezes per nostril of Flonase

### Shower

- Wear a swimsuit or clothes you don't mind getting wet
- Brush teeth and mouthwash in shower
- Clean stomach Mickey button area well with split sponge

### After Shower

1. Transfer back to bed on top of a towel
2. If comfortable, assist with men's health and wellness routine
3. Sponge bath
4. Put on [catheter](../medical/equipment/catheter.md)
5. Get dressed (catheter tube goes down inside right leg of pants)

### Transfer to Wheelchair

1. Unplug wheelchair and feeding machine
2. Turn on wheelchair and tilt back
3. Transfer Owen to sling → lift → wheelchair
4. Adjust clothes and catheter

### Kitchen - Feeding Setup

1. Reattach feeding tube to Owen's stomach Mickey button
2. Give Owen **6.6 mL Evrysdi** (use orange syringe) through feeding tube, followed by a tall beaker of water
3. Prepare [feeding formula](../feeding/feeding.md):
   - 1 pouch Liquid Hope
   - 1 pouch water
   - 2 boxes Kate Farms
   - 2 boxes water
   - Fill bag completely to the very top *(secure cap well to prevent spills)*
4. Attach feeding bag to carabiner on wheelchair
5. Attach to feeding machine in black bag
6. Turn on (hold button until numbers stop changing)
7. Prime until food reaches tip
8. Attach to feeding tube and press "Run"

### Final Morning Tasks

- Turn on computer
- Clean all syringes and beakers with warm water (no soap)
- Empty humidifier canister → leave to dry in bathroom
- Empty suction canister → rinse with warm water and soap → return to machine (or wash in dishwasher)
- Make beds, tidy bathrooms/bedrooms/kitchen
- Complete all [shift responsibilities](../household/chores.md)
