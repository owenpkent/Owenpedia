# Night/Sleep Routine

> **Hours:** 10:00 PM - 7:00 AM

---

## Baby Monitor

**Critical:** Make sure baby monitor is turned on and volume is up while you are sleeping.

- One monitor stays in Owen's bedroom next to bed
- One monitor stays in the caregiver room on nightstand

---

## Responding to Calls

Go to Owen's room when he calls out during the night.

### Common Requests

Owen may want any or all of the following:
- Covers adjusted
- Suction
- Turned over
- Legs/arms adjusted
- Pillows adjusted

### Frequency

He may wake up **1-5 times** during the night and need assistance with these things.
