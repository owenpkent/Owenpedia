## Quick Start for New Caregivers

**Day 1 Orientation:**
1. Read [Employee Handbook](./employee-handbook.md)
2. Review [Job Description](./job-description.md)
3. Study [Morning Shift Guide](../care/morning-shift-guide.md)
4. Study [Evening Shift Guide](../care/evening-shift-guide.md)

**Week 1 Focus:**
- Learn morning and evening routines
- Understand equipment operation
- Practice transfers with supervision
- Review medication administration
- Familiarize with household responsibilities

## Core Values

**1. Presence Over Perfection**
- Be fully present during shifts
- Quality of care matters more than checking boxes

**2. Joy in the Journey**
- Find moments of connection and laughter
- Help Owen with projects and creative endeavors

**3. Authenticity & Respect**
- Respect Owen's autonomy and preferences
- Maintain professional boundaries while being personable

**4. Flexibility & Problem-Solving**
- Adapt to changing needs
- Ask questions when unsure

---

## Essential Shift Information

### Morning Shift: 7:00 AM - 12:00 PM
- Allow 2 hours for morning care routine
- Owen will talk you through his routine
- No breakfast - Owen does not eat in the morning
- All shift responsibilities must be completed by noon

### Evening Shift: 5:00 PM - 10:00 PM + Overnight
- Bring something yummy to share for dinner (~1 cup of food)
- Owen may wake 1-5 times during night needing assistance
- Baby monitor must be on with volume up while sleeping
- Be ready for morning shift handoff at 7am
