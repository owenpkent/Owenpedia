**2. Joy in the Journey**

## The Essentials

### Hygiene & Safety

**Health Protocols:**
- **Wash your hands** before and after every contact with Owen
- **Always wear a mask** when around Owen and in the kitchen (except when eating or drinking)
- **Sanitize surfaces** at the beginning and end of every shift
- Stay home if you're feeling unwell - communicate immediately

**Safety First:**
- Always use [lift and sling](../medical/equipment.md#lift-sling) for transfers - never attempt manual transfers
- Check all [equipment](../medical/equipment.md) before use
- Report any equipment malfunctions immediately
- Know emergency contacts and procedures

### Household Responsibilities

**The Golden Rule: Do the Dishes**
- This is essential! Clean kitchen = happy household
- See [Household Checklists](../care/household-checklists.md) for complete daily/weekly/monthly tasks

**Daily Standards:**
- All shift responsibilities completed before leaving
- Kitchen clean and tidy
- Beds made, rooms organized
- Equipment cleaned and maintained

### Scheduling & Communication

**Scheduling:**
- All scheduling requests go through Owen
- Must be confirmed **in writing**
- Provide as much advance notice as possible for time-off requests
- Communicate availability changes promptly

**Communication Standards:**
- Be proactive - don't wait for problems to escalate
- Document care activities accurately
- Share observations about Owen's comfort or concerns
- Coordinate with other shift caregivers during handoffs

## Professional Standards

### Boundaries & Ethics

**Maintain Professional Boundaries:**
- Be friendly, but maintain appropriate caregiver-client relationship
- Respect privacy and confidentiality
- No personal use of Owen's belongings without permission
- Keep personal issues separate from work

**Privacy & Dignity:**
- Owen's personal information is confidential
- Maintain dignity during all care activities
- Knock before entering rooms
- Respect personal space and preferences

**Transparency:**
- Be honest about mistakes or concerns
- Don't hide problems - address them
- Document accurately and truthfully
- See [Transparency of Care](../care/transparency-of-care.md) for complete standards

### Continuous Improvement

**Learning & Growth:**
- Ask for clarification on procedures you're unsure about
- Learn from experienced caregivers
- Stay updated on Owen's changing needs
- Suggest process improvements

**Feedback Welcome:**
- Owen will provide feedback on your care
- Take feedback constructively
- Share your own observations and suggestions
- Regular check-ins help everyone improve
