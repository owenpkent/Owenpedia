# Live-In Caregiver Job Description

---

## Position Overview

The Live-In Caregiver provides daily personal care and household support for Owen.

---

## Responsibilities

### Personal Care
- Repositioning at night as needed
- Getting Owen up in the morning
- Personal care (shaving, etc.)
- Feeding Owen dinner

### Household
- Preparing dinners
- Taking out trash, recycling, and yard waste
- Doing all dishes before bed
- Opening boxes and putting contents away on the day they arrive
- Deep cleaning once a week
- Laundry twice a week

### Transportation
- Taking Owen to appointments and social outings as needed

---

## Compensation

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Hourly Rate | $15/hour |
| Hours per Day | 18 hours |
| Days per Week | 4 days |
| Monthly Pay | ~$4,700 |

### Housing
- Private room provided
- Rent: $600/month (utilities included)

---

## Rules

1. **Guests:** Have minimal guests over and please let Owen know when you are having someone over
2. **Punctuality:** Please be on time, especially when getting Owen up in the morning for meetings
3. **Substances:** Cannot be intoxicated while working (drugs or alcohol)
4. **Illegal Items:** Do not bring anything illegal on the premises (drugs, fireworks, etc.)
