## Live-In Caregiver Position

**Location:** Loveland, CO

### Position Summary
Seeking a compassionate and reliable live-in caregiver to provide personal care and household support for Owen, a wheelchair user with muscular dystrophy.

### Responsibilities
- Personal care assistance (morning routine, transfers, feeding)
- Nighttime repositioning as needed
- Meal preparation and feeding
- Household tasks (cleaning, laundry, dishes)
- Transportation to appointments and social outings

### Compensation & Benefits
- **Pay:** $15/hour
- **Hours:** 18 hours/day, 4 days/week (~$4,700/month)
- **Housing:** Private room included
- **Rent:** $600/month (utilities included)

### Requirements
- Reliable and punctual
- Compassionate and respectful
- Able to perform physical tasks (lifting with equipment)
- Clean background check
- Must not be intoxicated while working
