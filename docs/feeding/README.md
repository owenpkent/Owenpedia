## Quick Reference

### Formula Mix (Standard)
- 1 pouch Liquid Hope
- 1 pouch water
- 2 boxes Kate Farms
- 2 boxes water

**Fill feeding bag completely to the top**

### Feeding Rate
150-180 milliliters per hour

### Medication Administration
- **Evrysdi:** 6.6 mL daily (use orange syringe)
- Given through feeding tube in morning
