# Chicken Bake with Ricotta Topping

Baked chicken cutlets with marinara and creamy ricotta topping.

---

## Ingredients

### For the Chicken
- 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breast (thin cutlets)
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- 2 cups marinara sauce
- ¼ cup grated parmesan cheese (divided)
- ½ cup mozzarella cheese

### For the Ricotta Topping
- 15 oz ricotta cheese
- ¼ cup grated parmesan cheese
- 1 large egg
- 2 tbsp freshly chopped parsley
- ¼ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp black pepper

---

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F
2. Season chicken cutlets with Italian seasoning and garlic powder
3. In a bowl, mix ricotta, parmesan, egg, parsley, salt, and pepper
4. Spread half the marinara sauce in a baking dish
5. Arrange chicken cutlets on top
6. Spread ricotta mixture over chicken
7. Top with remaining marinara sauce
8. Sprinkle with mozzarella and remaining parmesan
9. Bake for 25-30 minutes until chicken is cooked through and cheese is bubbly
