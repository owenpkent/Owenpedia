# Chicken Parmigiana

Breaded and fried chicken cutlets with tomato sauce and melted cheese.

**Total Time:** 1 hour 15 minutes  
**Prep:** 30 minutes  
**Cook:** 45 minutes  
**Yield:** 4 servings

---

## Ingredients

### For the Chicken
- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, pounded thin
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper
- 2 cups all-purpose flour, seasoned with salt and pepper
- 4 large eggs, beaten with 2 tbsp water and seasoned with salt and pepper
- 2 cups panko bread crumbs
- 1 cup vegetable oil or pure olive oil
- 1 lb fresh mozzarella, thinly sliced
- ¼ cup freshly grated parmesan
- Fresh basil or parsley leaves for garnish

### For the Tomato Sauce
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 large Spanish onion, finely chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, smashed with kosher salt to make a paste
- Two 28-ounce cans plum tomatoes with juices, puréed in blender
- One 16-ounce can crushed tomatoes
- 1 small can tomato paste
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 small bunch Italian parsley
- 1 Cubano chile pepper, chopped
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper

---

## Instructions

### Make the Tomato Sauce
1. Heat olive oil in medium saucepan over medium heat
2. Add onions and garlic, cook until soft
3. Add puréed tomatoes with juices, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, 1 cup water, bay leaf, parsley, and Cubano pepper
4. Bring to a boil
5. Season to taste with salt and pepper
6. Reduce heat and cook until slightly thickened, about 30 minutes

### Prepare the Chicken
1. Preheat oven to 400°F
2. Season chicken on both sides with salt and pepper
3. Dredge each breast in seasoned flour, tap off excess
4. Dip in egg mixture, let excess drip off
5. Dredge on both sides in bread crumbs
6. Divide oil between 2 large sauté pans
7. Heat over high heat until almost smoking
8. Add 2 chicken breasts to each pan
9. Cook until golden brown on both sides, about 2 minutes per side
10. Transfer to baking sheet
11. Top each breast with tomato sauce, mozzarella slices, salt, pepper, and 1 tbsp parmesan
12. Bake until chicken is cooked through and cheese is melted, 5-7 minutes
13. Remove from oven and garnish with basil or parsley
