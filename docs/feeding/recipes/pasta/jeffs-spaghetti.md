# Jeff's Spaghetti

A simple, flavorful tuna pasta with olives and wine.

---

## Ingredients

- 2 cans tuna in oil
- ¾ cup olive oil
- 3 oz pitted olives
- 3 oz grated parmesan cheese
- Basil
- Parsley
- Red pepper flakes
- Black pepper
- Garlic
- ½ cup white wine
- Pasta (spaghetti or your choice)

---

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large pan
2. Add garlic, cook until golden
3. Add olives and seasonings (basil, parsley, red pepper flakes, black pepper)
4. Add mostly drained tuna, breaking it up
5. Add white wine
6. Simmer for 5 minutes
7. Add grated parmesan cheese
8. Toss with cooked pasta
