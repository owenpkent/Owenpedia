# Oven-Baked Salmon and Sunshine Rice

Salmon baked on a bed of curried rice with cauliflower and tomatoes.

**Total Time:** 1 hour 20 minutes  
**Servings:** 4

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup long-grain brown rice
- 2 cups small cauliflower florets
- 1 small yellow onion, chopped
- 2 tsp curry powder
- 1 can no-salt-added, fire-roasted diced tomatoes with liquid
- 1 lemon, halved (divided)
- ¾ lb skinless salmon fillet, pin bones removed, cut into large chunks
- 2 tbsp chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley or cilantro
- 1 tsp coconut oil
- ½ tsp fine sea salt (divided)

---

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F
2. Heat coconut oil in large Dutch oven over medium heat
3. Add rice and toast, stirring often, until fragrant, 3-4 minutes
4. Stir in cauliflower, 1½ cups water, onion, curry powder, ¼ tsp salt, tomatoes with liquid, and juice from half the lemon
5. Cover tightly with foil and Dutch oven lid
6. Bake until rice is almost tender, 1 to 1¼ hours
7. Season salmon on all sides with remaining ¼ tsp salt
8. Arrange salmon in single layer on top of rice
9. Continue to bake, uncovered, until salmon is just cooked through, about 10 minutes more
10. Garnish with parsley or cilantro
11. Cut remaining lemon half into wedges and serve alongside to squeeze over the top

---

## Notes

- Check rice at 1 hour to avoid overcooking
- Can substitute quinoa for rice (reduce cooking time)
- Leftovers reheat well
