# Salmon Burgers

Quick and flavorful canned salmon patties.

**Yield:** 3-4 burgers

---

## Ingredients

- 14 oz can salmon, drained
- 1 egg, lightly beaten
- ½ cup breadcrumbs
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- Zest and juice of 1 lemon
- 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- ½ tsp red pepper flakes
- Salt to taste
- Black pepper to taste
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for cooking)

### To Serve
- Hamburger buns
- Romaine lettuce
- Red onion, sliced

---

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, combine drained salmon, egg, breadcrumbs, garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, dill, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper
2. Mix well
3. Form into 3-4 patties
4. Heat olive oil in skillet over medium heat
5. Cook patties until golden brown, about 3-4 minutes each side
6. Serve on hamburger buns with romaine lettuce and red onion

---

## Tips

- Don't overmix - keep some texture
- Can be made with fresh salmon too
- Great with tartar sauce or aioli
