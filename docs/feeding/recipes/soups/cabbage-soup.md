# Cabbage Soup

A hearty, healthy vegetable soup that's perfect for meal prep.

---

## Ingredients

- 6 green onions, chopped
- 2 green peppers, chopped
- 1 large can diced tomatoes
- 3 carrots, sliced
- 10 oz mushrooms, sliced
- 1 bunch celery, chopped
- Half a head of cabbage, chopped
- 2 bouillon cubes
- Black pepper to taste
- Onion powder to taste
- Garlic to taste
- Parsley to taste
- Tomato paste (to thicken, optional)
- 12 cups water

---

## Instructions

1. Chop all vegetables
2. Add all ingredients to a large pot
3. Add 12 cups water
4. Stir to combine
5. Cover and put heat on low
6. Simmer for at least 2 hours
7. Taste and adjust seasonings as needed
8. Add tomato paste if you want a thicker soup

---

## Notes

- This soup freezes well
- Great for batch cooking
- Can add beans or lentils for extra protein
- Flavor improves after a day in the refrigerator
