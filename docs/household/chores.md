# Chores Schedule

---

## Morning Shift

### Daily Tasks

- [ ] Dishwasher unloaded
- [ ] Boxes opened, put away, broken down
- [ ] Trash and recycling emptied as needed

### Weekly Tasks

| Day | Tasks |
|-----|-------|
| **Sunday** | Laundry, water air plants |
| **Monday** | Wash towels, water venus fly trap |
| **Tuesday** | Clean breathing tube |
| **Wednesday** | Vacuum floors, water air plants |
| **Thursday** | Change sheets |
| **Friday** | Start van (run 10 min), water tree |
| **Saturday** | Clean refrigerator |

### Monthly Tasks

| When | Task |
|------|------|
| 1st of month | New [catheter](../medical/equipment/catheter.md) tube |
