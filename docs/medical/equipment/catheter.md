# Catheter Care

Owen uses an external (condom) catheter system.

---

## Components

| Part | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Condom Catheter | External catheter worn during day |
| Extension Tube | Longer tube connecting to collection |
| Pee Jug | Collection container |
| Office Mat | Placed under pee jug |

---

## Morning - Putting On

1. After sponge bath, put on catheter
2. Get Owen dressed
3. Route catheter tube down inside right leg of pants
4. Connect to pee jug system

---

## Nighttime - Removal

1. Remove catheter after transferring Owen to bed
2. **Discard** the condom catheter (single use)
3. **Rinse** the longer extension tube with warm water
4. **Hang** in shower to dry overnight

---

## Pee Jug Maintenance

### Nightly (After Owen is in Bed)

1. Empty into toilet
2. Rinse jug out
3. Wipe outside of jug with disinfecting wipe
4. Wipe mat with disinfecting wipe
5. Wipe any spots on floor around mat
6. Fill jug â…“ full of water
7. Replace on office mat

### Weekly

- Replace pee jug (Friday evening task)

---

## Monthly Replacement

- New catheter tube on the **1st of each month**
