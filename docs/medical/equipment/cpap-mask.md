# CPAP Mask

## Overview

The CPAP mask delivers air from the ventilator system to support breathing during sleep.

---

## Specifications

- **Type:** Nasal CPAP mask
- **Purpose:** Delivers air from ventilator system

---

## Usage

### Application
- Put CPAP mask on Owen at bedtime after all breathing machines are turned on
- Ensure proper fit and seal
- Adjust straps as needed for comfort

### Removal
- Remove mask in the morning after turning off breathing machines
- Clean immediately after removal

---

## Maintenance

### Daily Cleaning
- Clean nose mask nightly after removal
- Rinse with warm water
- Allow to air dry

### Replacement Schedule
- **New mask every 3 months**
- Check for wear, cracks, or loss of seal
- Replace immediately if damaged
