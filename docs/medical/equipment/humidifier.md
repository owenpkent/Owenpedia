# Humidifier

## Overview

The humidifier adds moisture to the air from the ventilator system, preventing dryness and improving comfort during sleep.

---

## Specifications

- **Location:** Middle of bedside equipment (between ventilator and oxygen)
- **Settings:** "Low" (only one of three lights on)

---

## Usage

### Purpose
Adds moisture to air from ventilator to prevent dryness in airways and improve breathing comfort.

### Operation

**Bedtime Setup:**
1. Fill humidifier canister with cool tap water up to the fill line
2. Place canister in humidifier on bottom shelf next to bed
3. Make sure the 2 blue tubes are attached to the humidifier canister
4. Turn on after ventilator, before oxygen
5. Set to "Low" - only one of three lights should be on

**Turn-On Order:**
1. Ventilator (left) - first
2. **Humidifier (middle)** - second
3. Oxygen (right) - last

**Turn-Off Order:**
1. Oxygen (right) - first
2. **Humidifier (middle)** - second
3. Ventilator (left) - last

---

## Maintenance

### Daily
- Empty canister in the morning
- Leave canister out to dry in bathroom
- Rinse with clean water

### Cleaning
- Clean with warm water (no soap)
- Check for mineral buildup
- Replace canister as needed
