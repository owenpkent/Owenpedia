# Lift Sling

## Overview

The Universal Mesh Sling with Head Support enables safe transfers between bed, toilet, shower, and wheelchair using the patient lift system.

---

## Specifications

- **Model:** Universal Mesh Sling with Head Support - Small
- **Manufacturer:** Bestcare Lifts (Model SL-UMH831)
- **Type:** Coil lift sling
- **Product Link:** [Medical eShop](https://www.medicaleshop.com/universal-mesh-sling-with-head-support-small)

---

## Features

### Design
- **U-shaped construction** for easy placement
- **6-8 hook-up straps** for adjustable positioning
- **Mesh fabric** - washable and quick-drying
- **Split leg design** for toileting access
- **Integrated head support** for safety and comfort

### Purpose
Enables safe transfers between:
- Bed
- Toilet
- Shower
- Wheelchair

---

## Usage

### Transfer Protocol
1. Position sling properly under Owen
2. Attach sling straps to patient lift hooks
3. Ensure all connections are secure
4. Lift slowly and smoothly
5. Transfer to destination
6. Lower carefully

### Safety
- **Always use lift and sling for transfers**
- **Never attempt manual transfers**
- Check sling for wear before each use
- Ensure proper positioning before lifting

---

## Care & Maintenance

### Cleaning
- Washable fabric
- Machine wash as needed
- Quick-drying mesh material

### Inspection
- Check for wear regularly
- Inspect straps and connections
- Replace if showing signs of damage
- Check stitching and fabric integrity
