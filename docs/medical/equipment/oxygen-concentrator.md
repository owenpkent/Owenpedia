# Oxygen Concentrator

## Overview

The oxygen concentrator provides supplemental oxygen by concentrating oxygen from ambient air for respiratory support.

---

## Specifications

- **Type:** Oxygen Concentrator
- **Location:** Right side of bed
- **Function:** Concentrates oxygen from ambient air

---

## Usage

### Purpose
Provides supplemental oxygen to support breathing during sleep.

### Operation

**Turn-On Order (Bedtime):**
1. Ventilator (left) - first
2. Humidifier (middle) - second
3. **Oxygen (right)** - last to turn on

**Turn-Off Order (Morning):**
1. **Oxygen (right)** - first to turn off
2. Humidifier (middle) - second
3. Ventilator (left) - last

---

## Maintenance

- Keep air intake vents clear
- Check tubing for kinks or damage
- Report any unusual sounds or performance issues
- Ensure proper airflow around unit
