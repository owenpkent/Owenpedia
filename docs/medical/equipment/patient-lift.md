# Patient Lift

## Overview

The patient lift system works with the lift sling to enable safe transfers between bed, toilet, shower, and wheelchair.

---

## Specifications

- **Type:** Electric patient lift
- **Battery:** Rechargeable, charge as needed
- **Purpose:** Safe transfers using sling

---

## Usage

### Transfer Procedure

**Preparation:**
1. Ensure lift battery is charged
2. Position lift near transfer location
3. Attach sling to lift hooks securely
4. Check all connections

**Lifting:**
1. Use lift controls to raise Owen slowly
2. Ensure smooth, controlled movement
3. Keep Owen centered and balanced
4. Move to destination location

**Lowering:**
1. Position over destination (bed, wheelchair, toilet, shower chair)
2. Lower slowly and carefully
3. Ensure proper seating/positioning
4. Disconnect sling straps

---

## Safety Protocols

### Critical Safety Rules
- **Always use lift and sling for transfers**
- **Never attempt manual transfers**
- Check equipment before every use
- Ensure all connections are secure
- Move slowly and smoothly
- Communicate with Owen throughout transfer

### Pre-Transfer Checklist
- [ ] Battery charged
- [ ] Sling properly positioned
- [ ] All straps securely attached
- [ ] Destination area clear
- [ ] Owen ready for transfer

---

## Maintenance

### Daily
- Check battery level
- Inspect for any damage or issues
- Test controls before first use

### Charging
- Charge battery as needed
- Don't let battery fully deplete
- Keep charger accessible

### Inspection
- Check hydraulics/electric system
- Inspect wheels and mobility
- Test all controls regularly
- Report any malfunctions immediately

---

## Troubleshooting

**Lift Won't Operate:**
- Check battery charge
- Ensure power switch is on
- Check all connections

**Unusual Sounds:**
- Stop use immediately
- Report to Owen
- Contact supplier for service
