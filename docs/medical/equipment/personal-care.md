# Personal Care Equipment

## Hot Octopus

### Overview
The Hot Octopus is a personal wellness device designed for accessibility and ease of use.

**Product Information:** [Hot Octopus](https://www.hotoctopuss.com/)

### Usage
- Used during morning and/or evening routines
- Caregiver assistance provided if comfortable
- Privacy and dignity maintained at all times

### Care & Maintenance
- Clean according to manufacturer instructions
- Store in designated location
- Ensure device is charged as needed

### Caregiver Guidelines
- Only assist if Owen is comfortable with assistance
- Leave the room to provide privacy
- Maintain professional boundaries
- Respect Owen's autonomy and preferences
