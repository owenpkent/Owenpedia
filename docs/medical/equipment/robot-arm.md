# Robot Arm

## Overview

The Kinova Jaco V2 robotic arm assists Owen with tasks and activities throughout the day, providing increased independence.

---

## Specifications

- **Model:** Kinova Jaco V2
- **Type:** Assistive robotic arm
- **Purpose:** Assists with daily tasks and activities
- **Power:** Electric, turn off at bedtime
- **Supplier:** Partners in Medicine
- **Contact:** Nathaniel Swenson

---

## Usage

### Daily Operation

**During Day:**
- Owen operates robot arm independently
- Used for various tasks and activities
- Assists with reaching and manipulation

**Bedtime:**
- Turn off robot arm at bedtime (9:00 PM)
- Part of evening shutdown routine

---

## Power Management

### Daily Schedule
- On during waking hours
- **Turn off at bedtime** as part of evening routine
- Turn back on in morning as needed
