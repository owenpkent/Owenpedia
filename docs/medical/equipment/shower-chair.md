# Shower Chair

## Overview

The shower chair provides seated support during showering, enabling safe and comfortable bathing.

---

## Specifications

- **Purpose:** Seated shower support
- **Location:** Bathroom shower stall
- **Use:** Daily during morning routine

---

## Usage

### Morning Shower Routine

**Transfer to Shower Chair:**
1. Use lift and sling to transfer Owen from toilet to shower chair
2. Position securely in shower stall
3. Ensure Owen is comfortable and stable

**During Shower:**
- Caregiver assists with showering (wear swimsuit or clothes you don't mind getting wet)
- Give medications during shower (Albuterol, Zyrtec, Flonase)
- Brush teeth and mouthwash in shower
- Clean stomach Mickey button area well with split sponge

**After Shower:**
1. Use lift and sling to transfer Owen back to bed
2. Place on towel for drying

---

## Safety

### Transfer Safety
- Always use lift and sling for transfers to/from shower chair
- Never attempt manual transfers
- Ensure chair is stable before beginning shower
- Check that all equipment is secure

### During Use
- Ensure Owen is comfortable and secure
- Monitor for any slipping or instability
- Keep shower area clear of obstacles

---

## Maintenance

### Daily
- Rinse chair after each use
- Check for water damage or rust
- Ensure all parts are secure

### Regular Inspection
- Check stability and structural integrity
- Inspect seat and back support
- Ensure no sharp edges or damage
- Replace if showing signs of wear
