# Suction Machine

## Overview

The suction machine clears secretions from mouth and throat, used as needed especially during the night.

---

## Specifications

- **Purpose:** Clears secretions from mouth/throat
- **Use:** As Owen requests, especially at night

---

## Usage

### When to Use
- As Owen requests
- Especially needed at night
- When secretions need to be cleared

### Operation
- Use suction wand as directed by Owen
- Keep suction bottle nearby during night shifts

---

## Maintenance

### Daily Care
- Keep canister empty or no more than half full
- Empty and rinse with warm water and soap daily
- Can wash canister in dishwasher as needed

### Suction Water Bottle
- Keep water in hanging suction water bottle
- Add a splash of mouthwash to water to keep clean
- Refill as needed

### Cleaning Schedule
- Rinse canister daily with warm water and soap
- Return to suction machine after cleaning
- Dishwasher safe for deeper cleaning

---

## Troubleshooting

**Low Suction:**
- Check that canister is not too full
- Ensure all connections are secure
- Check for clogs in tubing

**Canister Full:**
- Empty immediately if more than half full
- Rinse and return to machine
