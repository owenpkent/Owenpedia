# Ventilator

## Overview

The Trilogy Ventilator provides respiratory support during sleep, assisting with breathing throughout the night.

---

## Specifications

- **Model:** Trilogy Ventilator
- **Type:** Portable ventilator for respiratory support
- **Location:** Left side of bed

---

## Usage

### Purpose
Assists breathing during sleep by providing controlled ventilation support.

### Operation

**Bedtime (Turn On First):**
1. Turn on Ventilator (left machine)
2. Then turn on Humidifier (middle)
3. Finally turn on Oxygen (right)

**Morning (Turn Off Last):**
1. Turn off Oxygen (right machine)
2. Turn off Humidifier (middle)
3. Turn off Ventilator (left) - last to turn off

---

## Maintenance

- Check equipment before each use
- Report any unusual sounds or performance issues immediately
- Keep area around ventilator clear and dust-free
