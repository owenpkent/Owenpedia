# Wheelchair

## Overview

Owen's power wheelchair provides mobility and independence throughout the day.

---

## Specifications

- **Model:** Rovi A3 with lateral tilt
- **Seating:** Custom seat from Aspen Seating
- **Features:** Lateral tilt capability for pressure relief and positioning

---

## Usage

### Daily Operation

**Morning:**
1. Unplug wheelchair from charger
2. Turn on wheelchair
3. Tilt back before transfers
4. Transfer Owen using lift and sling

**Throughout Day:**
- Owen controls wheelchair independently
- Lateral tilt used for pressure relief and positioning
- Monitor battery level

**Evening:**
1. Plug in wheelchair to charge at bedtime
2. Turn off wheelchair

---

## Controls

- **Power:** Turn on before transfers, turn off at bedtime
- **Tilt Function:** Always tilt back before transfers for safety
- **Lateral Tilt:** Used for pressure relief and positioning throughout day

---

## Maintenance

### Daily
- Charge overnight every night
- Check that wheelchair is functioning properly
- Ensure controls are responsive

### Safety Checks
- Always tilt wheelchair back before transfers
- Ensure wheelchair is turned on before attempting transfers
- Check battery level regularly
