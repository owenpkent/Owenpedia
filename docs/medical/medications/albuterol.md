# Albuterol

## Overview

Albuterol is a bronchodilator that relaxes muscles in the airways and increases air flow to the lungs.

**Wikipedia:** [Salbutamol (Albuterol)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Salbutamol)

---

## Owen's Usage

### Dosage
- **1 dose** via inhaler
- **Timing:** Morning (during shower routine)

### Administration
- Given during morning shower routine
- Inhaler format

---

## Purpose

- Opens airways
- Improves breathing
- Bronchodilator for respiratory support
