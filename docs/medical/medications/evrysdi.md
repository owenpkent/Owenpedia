# Evrysdi (Risdiplam)

## Overview

Evrysdi (risdiplam) is a medication used to treat spinal muscular atrophy (SMA) in adults and children. It works by increasing the production of survival motor neuron (SMN) protein.

**Wikipedia:** [Risdiplam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Risdiplam)

---

## Owen's Usage

### Dosage
- **6.6 mL** daily
- **Timing:** Morning (in kitchen after transfer to wheelchair)

### Administration

1. Measure **6.6 mL** using the orange syringe
2. Attach large syringe to [feeding tube](../feeding.md)
3. Push medication through feeding tube
4. Follow with a tall beaker of water

### Storage
- **Must be refrigerated**
- Keep in refrigerator at all times

---

## Purpose

- Treatment for spinal muscular atrophy (SMA)
- Increases SMN protein production
- Helps maintain motor neuron function
