# Flonase (Fluticasone)

## Overview

Flonase (fluticasone propionate) is a corticosteroid nasal spray used to treat nasal symptoms such as congestion, sneezing, and runny nose caused by seasonal or year-round allergies.

**Wikipedia:** [Fluticasone propionate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fluticasone_propionate)

---

## Owen's Usage

### Dosage
- **2 squeezes per nostril**
- **Timing:** Morning (during shower routine)

### Administration
- Given during morning shower routine
- Nasal spray format

---

## Purpose

- Nasal allergy relief
- Reduces inflammation in nasal passages
- Helps with congestion and breathing
