# Glycopyrrolate (Glyco)

## Overview

Glycopyrrolate is an anticholinergic medication that reduces secretions in the body. It is commonly used to reduce saliva and respiratory secretions.

**Wikipedia:** [Glycopyrrolate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Glycopyrronium_bromide)

---

## Owen's Usage

### Dosage
- Standard dose
- **Timing:** Bedtime (9:00 PM)

### Administration
- Given at start of bedtime routine
- Oral medication

---

## Purpose

- Reduces saliva production
- Reduces respiratory secretions
- Helps keep airways clear during sleep
