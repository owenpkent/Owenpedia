# Melatonin

## Overview

Melatonin is a hormone naturally produced by the body that regulates sleep-wake cycles. Supplemental melatonin is used as a sleep aid.

**Wikipedia:** [Melatonin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Melatonin)

---

## Owen's Usage

### Dosage
- Standard dose
- **Timing:** Bedtime (9:00 PM)

### Administration
- Given at start of bedtime routine
- Oral medication

---

## Purpose

- Sleep aid
- Helps regulate sleep-wake cycle
- Promotes natural sleep onset
