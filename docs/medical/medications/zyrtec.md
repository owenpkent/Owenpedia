# Zyrtec (Cetirizine)

## Overview

Zyrtec (cetirizine) is an antihistamine used to relieve allergy symptoms such as watery eyes, runny nose, itching eyes/nose, and sneezing.

**Wikipedia:** [Cetirizine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cetirizine)

---

## Owen's Usage

### Dosage
- **1 tablet**
- **Timing:** Morning (during shower routine)

### Administration
- Given during morning shower routine
- Oral tablet

---

## Purpose

- Allergy relief
- Reduces histamine response
- Helps with seasonal allergies
