# Chicken Cacciatore

Classic Italian braised chicken with peppers, tomatoes, and wine.

**Total Time:** 55 minutes  
**Prep:** 15 minutes  
**Cook:** 40 minutes  
**Yield:** 4 servings

---

## Ingredients

- 4 chicken thighs
- 2 chicken breasts with skin and backbone, halved crosswise
- 2 tsp salt, plus more to taste
- 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper, plus more to taste
- ½ cup all-purpose flour (for dredging)
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 large red bell pepper, chopped
- 1 onion, chopped
- 3 garlic cloves, finely chopped
- ¾ cup dry white wine
- 1 (28-ounce) can diced tomatoes with juice
- ¾ cup reduced-sodium chicken broth
- 3 tbsp drained capers
- 1½ tsp dried oregano leaves
- ¼ cup coarsely chopped fresh basil leaves

---

## Instructions

1. Sprinkle chicken pieces with 1 tsp each of salt and pepper
2. Dredge chicken pieces in flour to coat lightly
3. Heat oil in large heavy sauté pan over medium-high heat
4. Add chicken pieces and sauté until brown, about 5 minutes per side (work in batches if needed)
5. Transfer chicken to a plate and set aside
6. Add bell pepper, onion, and garlic to the same pan
7. Sauté over medium heat until onion is tender, about 5 minutes
8. Season with salt and pepper
9. Add wine and simmer until reduced by half, about 3 minutes
10. Add tomatoes with juice, broth, capers, and oregano
11. Return chicken pieces to pan and turn to coat in sauce
12. Bring sauce to a simmer
13. Continue simmering over medium-low heat until chicken is cooked through:
    - 30 minutes for breast pieces
    - 20 minutes for thighs
14. Transfer chicken to a platter using tongs
15. If necessary, boil sauce until it thickens slightly, about 3 minutes
16. Spoon off any excess fat from sauce
17. Spoon sauce over chicken
18. Sprinkle with fresh basil and serve
