# Horbein Chocolate Sludge

Rich, decadent chocolate sauce perfect for ice cream or desserts.

**Yield:** Approximately 28 ounces

---

## Ingredients

- 1 lb + 2 oz semisweet chocolate (high-quality)*
- Â¼ cup good quality hot coffee (regular or decaf)
- 1 cup heavy whipping cream
- 3 tbsp butter (mostly salted - adjust to taste)
- 3 tbsp white sugar

*Note: Use high-quality chocolate for the best results.

---

## Instructions

### Melt the Chocolate
1. Use a double boiler or microwave on half-power
2. Watch carefully to prevent burning

### Add Coffee
1. Stir or whisk the hot coffee into the melted chocolate
2. Mix until as smooth as possible

### Heat Cream Mixture
1. In a separate saucepan, gently heat the heavy whipping cream, butter, and sugar
2. Heat just to boiling, whisking well

### Combine Mixtures
1. Stir the hot cream mixture into the chocolate/coffee mixture
2. Whisk until smooth

### Store
1. Pour into jars
2. Cap tightly
3. Refrigerate or freeze

---

## Serving Suggestions

- Warm and pour over ice cream
- Use as a dip for fruit
- Drizzle over brownies or cake
- Eat with a spoon (no judgment!)

---

## Notes

- Tom liked salt, so the recipe uses salted butter - adjust to your preference
- Keeps in refrigerator for 2 weeks
- Freezes well for up to 3 months
- Reheat gently in microwave or double boiler
