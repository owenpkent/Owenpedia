## Pasta Dishes

### Jeff's Spaghetti

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cans tuna in oil
- ¾ cup olive oil
- 3 oz pitted olives
- 3 oz grated parmesan
- Basil, parsley, red pepper flakes, pepper, garlic
- ½ cup white wine

**Instructions:**
1. Heat oil, add garlic, cook until golden
2. Add olives and seasonings
3. Add mostly drained tuna
4. Add wine, simmer 5 mins
5. Add cheese

## Rice Dishes

### Italian Sausage and Rice Casserole

**Ingredients:**
- 1.5 lbs ground beef
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 green pepper, diced
- 14.5 oz canned tomatoes
- 1 cup shredded carrots
- 2 cups jasmine rice
- 2.5 cups beef broth
- 2 tbsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tsp each: garlic powder, salt
- 1 cup mozzarella cheese

**Instructions:**
1. Brown and drain ground beef, place in large casserole dish
2. Mix in onion, pepper, tomatoes, carrots, rice, broth, and seasonings
3. Cover tightly with foil, bake at 400°F for 50-60 minutes
4. Remove foil, add cheese, bake 5 more minutes

### Crack Chicken Tacos

**Ingredients:**
- 2 lb chicken breast (cut into 2" pieces)
- 1 cup flour, 2 cups panko, 3 eggs
- 1 cup BBQ sauce, ½ cup brown sugar
- Juice of 3 limes, 1 tsp garlic powder
- ½ head cabbage, cilantro, ¼ cup ranch
- 8 small flour tortillas

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 425°F
2. Coat chicken in flour → eggs → panko
3. Bake 23-25 minutes until golden
4. Mix BBQ sauce, brown sugar, lime juice, garlic
5. Pour sauce over baked chicken
6. Make slaw: cabbage + cilantro + ranch + lime
7. Serve in tortillas

## Seafood

### Salmon Burgers

**Ingredients:**
- 14 oz canned salmon
- 1 egg, ½ cup breadcrumbs
- 1 garlic clove
- Zest and juice of 1 lemon
- 2 tbsp dill, 1 tbsp Dijon
- 1 tsp Worcestershire, ½ tsp red pepper flakes

**Instructions:**
1. Combine ingredients, form 3-4 patties
2. Heat oil, cook patties 3-4 min each side
3. Serve on buns with lettuce and red onion
