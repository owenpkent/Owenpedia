# CBD-Infused Body Butter

Luxurious homemade body butter with CBD and essential oils.

---

## Ingredients

- ¼ cup infused coconut oil
- ¼ cup jojoba oil
- ¼ cup shea butter
- ¼ cup cocoa butter
- 10-20 drops essential oils (e.g., tea tree, eucalyptus)

---

## Instructions

1. In a double boiler, melt the cocoa butter first (it takes the longest)
2. Once cocoa butter starts to melt, add shea butter
3. Continue melting until fully liquefied
4. Add infused coconut oil and jojoba oil
5. Stir to combine for about 20 minutes
6. Remove from heat
7. Stir in 10-20 drops of essential oils (like tea tree and eucalyptus)
8. Refrigerate for 20 minutes until it starts to firm up (but isn't fully solid)
9. Using an electric beater, whip for 10 minutes until light and fluffy
10. Transfer to jars
11. Refrigerate overnight to fully set

---

## Notes

- Store in cool place or refrigerator
- Will melt in hot temperatures
- Use within 6 months for best quality
- Great for dry skin, eczema, or general moisturizing
