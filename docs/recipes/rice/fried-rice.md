# Fried Rice / Rice Mess

A quick stir-fry with chicken and vegetables.

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cooked rice
- 1 chicken breast, marinated in teriyaki sauce
- ½ onion, diced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 carrot, diced
- 1 bell pepper, diced
- ½ cup broccoli florets
- ½ cup snow peas
- Soy sauce to taste
- Sesame oil (optional)
- Eggs (optional)

---

## Instructions

1. Cook chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
2. Heat oil in large wok or skillet over high heat
3. Add garlic and onion, stir-fry until fragrant
4. Add carrots, bell pepper, broccoli, and snow peas
5. Stir-fry vegetables until tender-crisp
6. Add cooked rice, breaking up any clumps
7. Add cooked chicken
8. Season with soy sauce and sesame oil
9. Optional: Push rice to side, scramble eggs, then mix in

---

## Tips

- Use day-old rice for best texture
- Keep heat high for authentic stir-fry
- Have all ingredients prepped before starting
