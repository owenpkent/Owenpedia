# Italian Sausage and Rice Casserole

A hearty one-dish meal with ground beef, rice, and vegetables.

---

## Ingredients

- 1.5 lbs ground beef
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 green pepper, diced
- 14.5 oz canned tomatoes (1 can)
- 1 cup shredded carrots
- 2 cups jasmine rice (uncooked)
- 2.5 cups beef broth
- 2 tbsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup mozzarella cheese
- Chopped parsley for garnish (optional)

---

## Instructions

1. Brown and drain the ground beef
2. Place ground beef in a large casserole dish
3. Mix in onion, green pepper, diced tomatoes, shredded carrots, rice, beef broth, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, and salt
4. Cover tightly with foil
5. Bake at 400Â°F for 50-60 minutes, or until rice is tender
6. Remove foil, add mozzarella cheese on top
7. Bake for 5 more minutes until cheese is melted
8. Garnish with chopped parsley (optional)
9. Serve warm

---

## Notes

- Check rice at 50 minutes to avoid overcooking
- Can substitute ground turkey or Italian sausage for beef
