# Crack Chicken Tacos

Crispy baked chicken with tangy BBQ-lime sauce and fresh slaw.

---

## Ingredients

### For the Chicken
- 2 lb chicken breast, cut into 2-inch pieces
- 1 cup flour
- 2 cups panko bread crumbs
- 3 eggs
- 2 tbsp water
- Salt and black pepper

### For the Sauce
- 1 cup BBQ sauce
- ½ cup brown sugar
- Juice of 3 limes
- 1 tsp garlic powder

### For the Slaw
- ½ head green cabbage, shredded
- Fresh cilantro, chopped
- ¼ cup ranch dressing
- Lime juice
- Salt to taste

### To Serve
- 8 small flour tortillas

---

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425°F
2. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper
3. Set up breading station:
   - Bowl 1: Flour
   - Bowl 2: Eggs whisked with water
   - Bowl 3: Panko bread crumbs
4. Coat chicken pieces in flour
5. Dredge in egg mixture
6. Coat with panko
7. Transfer to baking sheet
8. Season with salt and pepper
9. Bake until golden and crispy, 23-25 minutes
10. Let cool slightly, then transfer to large bowl

### Make the Sauce
1. In a saucepan, combine BBQ sauce, brown sugar, lime juice, and garlic powder
2. Heat and stir until combined
3. Pour sauce over baked chicken and toss to coat

### Make the Slaw
1. Combine shredded cabbage, cilantro, ranch dressing, lime juice, and salt
2. Toss well

### Assemble
1. Warm tortillas
2. Add chicken to each tortilla
3. Top with slaw
4. Serve immediately
