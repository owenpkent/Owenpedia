# Quinoa Black Bean Burrito Bowls

Healthy, filling vegetarian bowls with Mexican flavors.

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup quinoa
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 cans black beans, drained and rinsed
- ¼ cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- Chili powder to taste
- Cayenne pepper to taste
- Lime juice
- Salt to taste
- Shredded lettuce
- ½ cup water

---

## Instructions

1. Cook quinoa according to package directions
2. Heat olive oil in pan over medium heat
3. Sauté onions and garlic until softened
4. Add black beans to onions with ½ cup water
5. Add chili powder, cayenne, and salt
6. Simmer until heated through and slightly thickened
7. Fluff cooked quinoa with fork
8. Assemble bowls: quinoa base, black bean mixture, shredded lettuce
9. Top with cilantro and squeeze of lime juice

---

## Optional Toppings

- Avocado or guacamole
- Sour cream or Greek yogurt
- Salsa
- Shredded cheese
- Jalapeños
- Corn
