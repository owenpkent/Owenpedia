## Travel Considerations

### Medical Equipment
- Portable ventilator and power supplies
- CPAP equipment and backup supplies
- Suction machine (portable)
- Feeding pump and supplies
- Wheelchair charger and batteries

### Medications
**Critical:** Evrysdi must be refrigerated at all times
- Pack in insulated cooler with ice packs
- Plan for refrigeration at destination
- Bring all daily medications with extra days' supply

### Supplies
- Extra catheters and extension tubes
- Feeding bags and formula
- Syringes and beakers
- Cleaning supplies

---

## Pre-Travel Checklist

### 1 Week Before
- [ ] Confirm accommodations have refrigerator for Evrysdi
- [ ] Order extra supplies if needed
- [ ] Check equipment batteries and chargers
- [ ] Verify insurance coverage for travel

### 1 Day Before
- [ ] Pack all medications
- [ ] Charge all equipment
- [ ] Print emergency contacts
- [ ] Pack medical documentation

### Day of Travel
- [ ] Evrysdi in cooler with ice packs
- [ ] All equipment packed and charged
- [ ] Emergency contacts accessible
- [ ] Medical information cards
